Welcome To My Site!

Hello, my name is Menno and welcome to my little site on the internet. I've made this site because I love the internet and computers.

I live in the Netherlands in a city called Spijkenisse. If you want to know more about me, click the about button above.

If you find a more readable font-color let me know and I'll take a look at it!

One of my greatest passions is programming, which has become more than just a hobby; it's a way of life. I enjoy working with various programming languages, with Python, HTML, and CSS being my primary choices. They allow me to bring my creative ideas to life on the web.

When I'm not immersed in the digital world, I have other interests as well. I am an avid member of the scouting community, and I find joy in the adventures and camaraderie it brings. It's a perfect balance to my digital endeavors.

As a cat lover, I can't resist the charm of these feline companions. Their independence and playfulness make them the perfect pets, and they often inspire me in many ways.

I also once tried my hand at C++ programming, but I must admit, it was quite a challenge! Nevertheless, I'm always eager to learn and explore new horizons in the ever-evolving world of technology.
