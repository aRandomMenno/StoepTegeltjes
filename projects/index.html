RiMusic by Fast4x

This is a music player, which is just an application which can play any video/music from YouTube (Music)
It is a fork of ViMusic which is probably abandoned by it's creator/maintainer.
This is my primary music app, the app also caches any music played so you can play your favorite songs offline for free!
(In settings you can change the size of the cached songs so your device doesn't get filled up with only music.)
I switched to ViMusic first (from Spotify), but after I found RiMusic I immediately switched to it.
